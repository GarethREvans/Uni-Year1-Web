My Racing Setup

Thrustmaster T150

The Thrustmaster T150 is an official simulator for PLAYSTATION 4 and PLAYSTATION 3. The Thrustmaster T150 has a wheel that is 28cm in diameter, as well as 2 metal wheel-mounted sequential paddle shifters that are 13cm tall, and 13 action buttons + 1 D-Pad. The wheel has an angle of roation, up to 1080 degrees and has an adjustable force feedback system.

The pedals angle of inclination can also adjust and the brake pedal has progressive resistance aswell as having a wide foot rest.

I personally feel that the Thristmaster T150 is brilliant value for money. The wheel comes many of the features, more expensive wheels have, but for a much cheaper price. Although a clutch pedal isn't included and the pedals themselves are quite basic, they are comfortable to use and are very durable.

Wheel Stand Pro V2

The Wheel Stand Pro V2 is a metal frame that allows a pedal set and gaming wheel to be mounted together. The wheel platform allows a wheel to be hard mounted or mounted with the use of a clamp.

The stand is slim and easily adjustable, and can accomodate all sizes of users and chairs. It is ready to set up out of the box and is durable enough to last for the products full lifetime.

I feel that the wheel Stand Pro is an essential piece of equipment for when using a gaming wheel. Its practical and doesn't restrict the user to mounting a gaming wheel on to a desk or table (which when using a console , is usually not an option).
